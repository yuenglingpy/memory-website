Memory Machine

Memory Machine is an app to store, track and remember important events in your life.

Your data is stored locally on your device. You own it and control access.

> The Philosophy

Memory Machine is a local-first app for your life experience. It is a digital vault of curated, user controlled memories that lives on your hardware, not in the cloud.

> System Capabilities

* Encrypted Local Storage: Local device storage: You have complete control/visibility over your data.

* Recurring Memory Logic: Track variable dates (e.g., Thanksgiving) and annual events like birthdays.

* Journaling: Add standalone journal entries or connect them to your stored memories.

* Portability: Full export to JSON standard.

* Air-Gapped by Design: No internet connection required (External links require connectivity; Local vault works offline).
