A local-first, digital vault for your lifes memories.

Private Beta

Memory Machine is a desktop application that started because I wanted to remember the details of all of my camping trips. It's evolved from a spreadsheet into a tool for storing and remembering the moments that define our lives — birthdays, milestones, vacations, concerts, traditions, and everything in between.

System Capabilities
Memory Bank

A curated vault for the events that define your life. Vacations, milestones, concerts — organized, filtered, and searchable.

Recurring Events

Track dates that return — birthdays, anniversaries, traditions. Annual, biennial, or variable. The machine remembers with you.

Journal

Attach entries to memories or just write your thoughts down in a standalone entry. Rich text, mood tracking, linked to your timeline.

Local by Design

Designed with user privacy at the forefront. No accounts. No sync. No cloud. Your data lives on your hardware — exportable in open JSON whenever you want it.

Access

Memory Machine is currently private and available by invitation only.
